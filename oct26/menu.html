History... Where Homemade is the Difference

Homemade Berrocks

Our Specialty - delicious fillings wrapped in fresh dough & baked until golden brown. Available individually or they're cheaper by the half dozen & dozen.

  • Ground beef - w/ cabbage & onions
  • Shredded roast - w/ cabbage & onions
  • BBQ beef - lean shredded roast w/ tangy BBQ sauce
  • Mexican - seasoned ground beef, two cheeses & mild green chiles
  • Breakfast - scrambled eggs, sauteed onions, cheese...choose ham or bacon

Our berrocks are large, 8 oz. size and are baked fresh each day. You may special order half size berrocks in any variety, or hors d'oeuvre size berrocks in either the ground beef or shredded roast varieties. Minimum order is one dozen for these special order items and 24 hours notice is required.

Chicken Pot Pies

Fresh, tender chicken, diced potatoes & carrots, and creamy homemade gravy in our famous flaky crust.

Deluxe Salads

Choose chicken salad, chef, tuna chef or tri tip salad, all on crisp greens with your choice of Italian, pomegranate vinaigrette, or our homemade ranch, bleu cheese or thousand island dressing.
